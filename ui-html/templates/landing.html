System Information

Is NEXUS AI truly decentralized?

expand_more

Yes. The Nexus kernel runs on a peer-to-peer network of validator nodes. Governance is controlled by the NEXUS DAO, ensuring that no single entity has control over the operating system's evolution.

Can I migrate my existing smart contracts?

expand_more

Nexus is EVM-compatible and supports easy migration via our Auditor tool, which helps you wrap existing contracts in the Nexus security layer.

What chains does Nexus support?

expand_more

We currently support Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, and Solana natively, with more integrations planned for our Q4 Neural Mesh phase.
